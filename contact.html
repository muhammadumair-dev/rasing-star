Basketball Activity Detection

Introducing our cutting-edge basketball activity detection app, designed to revolutionize how you analyze and track basketball games. This app leverages advanced AI and computer vision technologies to detect basketballs, players, and crucial in-game moments like jumps and shot attempts with pinpoint accuracy.

Whether you're a coach, player, or enthusiast, our app provides real-time insights into game dynamics, helping you enhance performance and strategy. By detecting and recognizing key movements such as player jumps and shots, the app delivers actionable data for immediate feedback or post-game analysis. With a user-friendly interface, the app integrates seamlessly with video footage, allowing users to track every critical moment effortlessly. Take your basketball analysis to the next level with state-of-the-art technology that turns every game into a data-rich experience.
